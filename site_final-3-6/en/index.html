Refractive surgery in Brussels

Refractive surgery encompasses all the procedures that correct visual defects and reduce or eliminate dependence on glasses and contact lenses. Myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, presbyopia: for each of these conditions, there are today precise, well-documented surgical solutions, adapted to very different profiles.

The purpose of this website is simple: to help you understand what will happen during your procedure, and why. You will find here no commercial pitch and no promises, only honest explanations of how each operation works, the indications specific to each technique, recovery, the results one can expect, and the real risks.

Every eye is different. The surgical decision is not made based on a fashionable technique or an intuitive preference, but after a precise assessment of your corneal anatomy, your correction and your expectations. My role is to give you the information so that you can decide freely.
